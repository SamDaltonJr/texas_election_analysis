## ğŸ” Two Plans: PLANH2316 vs. PLANH2176

### Why Two Plans?

Texas redrew its House district boundaries after the 2020 Census:

- **PLANH2316** (2021): Original redistricting plan enacted by the Legislature
- **PLANH2176** (2021): Court-ordered plan after legal challenges

### Which Plan to Use?

**For Elections:**
- **2018**: Only PLANH2316 available (used pre-2020 Census boundaries)
- **2020**: Both plans have this data (2020 election used old boundaries, but results shown in both new plans for comparison)
- **2022, 2024**: Both plans - **PLANH2176 was actually used for these elections**

**Recommendation:**
- Use **PLANH2316** for 2018, 2020 (for consistency with your parsed 2018 data)
- Use **PLANH2176** for 2022, 2024 (this is the official plan used)
- Or use both and note the plan differences in your analysis

### Districtboundary Differences

The district boundaries changed slightly between plans. Some districts were renumbered or reshaped. The PLANH2176 plan was designed to better comply with the Voting Rights Act.
